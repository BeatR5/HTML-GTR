NISSAN SKYLINE GT-R R34

The Icon. The Legend. "Godzilla". Discover the car that defined a generation of performance.

The Legend of Godzilla

The Nissan Skyline GT-R R34 is an iconic Japanese sports car revered for its performance and distinctive design. Produced from 1999 to 2002, it is celebrated for its powerful twin-turbo RB26DETT engine, advanced all-wheel-drive system, and technologies like the ATTESA E-TS Pro that were ahead of their time. Its appearances in popular media solidified its status as a cult classic among car enthusiasts worldwide.

Unmatched Performance

The GT-R's technology and performance led the Australian motoring magazine *Wheels* to nickname it "Godzilla" back in 1989. The name stuck through all generations, with the R34 being described as "the best handling car we have ever driven." In tests, the R34 GT-R completed the quarter-mile (402m) in 12.2 seconds and accelerated from 0 to 100 km/h (0-62 mph) in just 4.4 seconds.

The R34's Enduring Legacy

The Nissan Skyline GT-R (日産・スカイラインGT-R) is more than just a car; it's a cultural phenomenon based on the Nissan Skyline.
