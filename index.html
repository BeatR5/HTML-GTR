The Legend of Godzilla

The Nissan Skyline GT-R R34 is an iconic Japanese sports car revered for its performance and distinctive design. Produced from 1999 to 2002, it is celebrated for its powerful twin-turbo RB26DETT engine, and its advanced all-wheel-drive system, the ATTESA E-TS Pro.

Unmatched Performance

The GT-R's technology led the Australian motoring magazine *Wheels* to nickname it "Godzilla". In tests, the R34 GT-R covered a quarter-mile in 12.2 seconds and accelerated from 0–100 km/h in a blistering 4.4 seconds, setting a new benchmark for sports cars.

The R34's Enduring Legacy

The Nissan Skyline GT-R (日産・スカイラインGT-R) is more than just a car; it's a cultural phenomenon.
