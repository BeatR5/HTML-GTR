The Skyline GT-R Legacy

Hakosuka: The Dawn of a Legend

1969-1972

This is where it all began. The first GT-R, known as "Hakosuka" (Boxy Skyline), established the legend by dominating on the racetrack.

R32: The Return of Godzilla

1989-1994

After a 16-year hiatus, the GT-R name returned with the R32. Its AWD system and RB26 engine dominated the tracks, earning it the "Godzilla" moniker.

R33: Refined Aerodynamics

1995-1998

R34: The Pinnacle of an Era

1999-2002

The apex of technology, design, and performance. The R34 became a pop culture icon with its aggressive looks and innovations like the MFD.

R35: The Modern Beast

2007-Present

Dropping the Skyline name but continuing the GT-R spirit, the R35 entered the scene as a technological marvel that redefined the supercar world.
