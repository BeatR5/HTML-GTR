V-spec II (BNR34) Technical Specifications

Engine & Power

Engine: 2.6L RB26DETT Twin-Turbo I6

Official Horsepower: 276 hp (Japanese gentlemen's agreement)

Actual Horsepower: ~330 hp

Torque: 392 Nm

Performance

Top Speed: 265 km/h (165 mph)

0-100 km/h (0-62 mph): ~4.6 seconds

Transmission: 6-speed Getrag manual

Weight: 1,560 kg

Exterior

Paint: Bayside Blue (and other iconic colors)

Wheels: 18-inch forged aluminum wheels

Body: Carbon fiber underbody diffuser, aggressive aerodynamic styling

Interior & Technology

Seats: Supportive racing-style bucket seats

Dashboard: Multi-Function Display (MFD) showing vital engine data

Drivetrain: ATTESA E-TS Pro AWD System
